[[def: key-event-log, key event log, KEL, KELs]]

~ A verifiable append-only log of [[ref: key events]] for an [[ref: AID]] that
~    is both backward and forward-chained, as defined by the
~    [KERI specification](https://trustoverip.github.io/kswg-keri-specification/).

~ More detail:
~    [WebOfTrust glossary](https://weboftrust.github.io/WOT-terms/docs/glossary/key-event-log).
