[[def: witness, witnesses]]

~ An entity that is designated by the [[ref: controller]] of an [[ref: AID]] to
~    verify, sign, and keep the key events associated with the AID, as defined by
~    the [KERI specification](https://trustoverip.github.io/kswg-keri-specification/).

~ Also see: [[xref: keri1, witness]] in the KERISuite glossary for more detail.
