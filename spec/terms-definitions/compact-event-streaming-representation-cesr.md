[[def: compact-event-streaming-representation, compact event streaming representation, CESR]]

~ An encoding format that enables round-trip text-binary conversion of
~    concatenated cryptographic primitives and general data types, as defined by
~    the [CESR specification](https://trustoverip.github.io/kswg-cesr-specification/) and
~    the [CESR Proof Signatures specification](https://trustoverip.github.io/tswg-cesr-proof-specification/draft-pfeairheller-cesr-proof.html).

~ More detail:
~    [WebOfTrust glossary](https://weboftrust.github.io/WOT-terms/docs/glossary/composable-event-streaming-representation).
